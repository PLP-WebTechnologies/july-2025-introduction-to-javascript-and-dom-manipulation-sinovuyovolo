About Us

Enkanyezini Livestock is a family-owned business deeply rooted in the community of Vryheid, KwaZulu-Natal. We are passionate about responsible farming practices and the well-being of our animals. Our mission is to supply local farmers and businesses with superior livestock that thrive in the South African climate.

Our Livestock

Cattle

Our cattle are known for their strong genetics and adaptability. We raise a variety of breeds suitable for both beef and dairy production, ensuring you get the best quality animals for your needs.

Goats

We provide healthy and robust goats for various purposes, including meat and milk production. Our goats are raised in a spacious, natural environment to ensure their well-being.

Sheep

Our sheep are carefully selected for their quality and resilience. Whether you're looking for wool or meat, our flock offers exceptional characteristics and health.
